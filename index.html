About the Film

Chicago Cafe is a documentary about the oldest Chinese restaurant in America—and the family who kept it going for over 100 years. When chef-owner Paul Fong steps into the spotlight, he must decide what to do with a legacy that spans three generations, shaped by immigration, resilience, and community. As he nears retirement, the question looms: what happens next?
